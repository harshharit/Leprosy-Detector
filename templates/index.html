Leprosy unlike most illness which arouses not only fear of infection – leading to aversion and social ostracism. This is called the stigma of leprosy and includes social rejection,loss of eployment and banishment.Rather than corner out someone support them help them because that's what community does.

It's something important you want to know.

Leprosy primarily affects your skin and nerves outside your brain and spinal cord, called the peripheral nerves. It may also strike your eyes and the thin tissue lining the inside of your nose.

He doesn’t have leprosy, but he’s discriminated all the same. There is a chance he’ll never get married. about us »

Leprosy is a chronic infectious disease . The disease is characterized by long incubation period generally 5-7 years . Leprosy is a leading cause of permanent physical disability. Timely diagnosis and treatment of cases, before nerve damage occurred, is the most effective way of preventing disability due to leprosy. So, we at Leprosy Detector attempts to not only diagnose and battle leprosy but also inform people about it.
